AI for Drug Discovery: A Journey into Machine Learning & Pharma

The pharmaceutical industry is undergoing a transformation, with AI-powered drug discovery at the forefront. Companies like DeepMind, Isomorphic Labs, and GSK are leveraging machine learning to accelerate drug design, reduce costs, and uncover novel therapeutics.

Why This Blog?

The goal of this blog is to:

  • Bridge the gap between life sciences and AI.
  • Provide step-by-step tutorials on real-world drug discovery projects.
  • Build an industry-relevant portfolio for AI-driven biotech roles.
  • Showcase how machine learning can revolutionize drug discovery.

Each project will be hands-on, with code, datasets, and implementation details that you can follow along with.

Project Focus: AI for Drug and Biologics Design

The first project will explore AI-driven drug and biologics design, focusing on small molecules, peptides, and antibodies. The goal is to predict drug-protein interactions and optimize biologics for therapeutic use.

Why This Project?

  • Small molecules are the foundation of most drug therapies, but discovering effective ones is time-consuming and expensive.
  • Peptides & biologics (such as antibodies) are increasingly important in modern medicine due to their high specificity and reduced side effects.
  • AI-driven modeling can predict interactions, generate novel candidates, and optimize molecular designs much faster than traditional methods.

Tools & Techniques

  • DeepChem – AI-driven molecular modeling.
  • AlphaFold – Predicting protein structures.
  • RDKit & AutoDock Vina – Molecular docking simulations.
  • ProteinMPNN & DiffDock – Peptide and protein-ligand interaction modeling.
  • Google Colab – Free cloud computing for running ML models.

Goals & Timeline

  • Week 1-2: Understanding molecular representations & AI models.
  • Week 3-4: Predicting drug-protein binding affinities with ML.
  • Week 5-6: Designing AI-generated peptide therapeutics.

This project will result in:

  • A trained AI model for small molecule binding prediction.
  • A pipeline for generating novel peptide binders.
  • A step-by-step tutorial & GitHub repository for others to follow.

Upcoming Projects

This first project will set the foundation for three additional AI-driven drug discovery projects:

  • ML for Multi-Omics Target Discovery – Identifying novel drug targets from large omics datasets.
  • Generative AI for De Novo Drug Design – AI-generated molecules optimized for drug-like properties.
  • AI-Driven Screening & Optimization – Refining candidate drugs with computational methods.
