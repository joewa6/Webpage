Welcome to My Projects Showcase

Published on: 2024-01-01

Hello and welcome to my personal webpage! My name is Joe Wallace, and I'm a postdoctoral researcher in computational chemistry. I've created this website as a platform to document and share my work, experiences, and the various projects I've been involved in over the years.

Why This Webpage?

As someone deeply involved in computational chemistry, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of interesting projects, each offering unique challenges and learning experiences. This website serves as a space where I can not only document these projects for my own records but also share them with fellow researchers, potential collaborators, and anyone interested in the field.

What to Expect

In the Projects section, you'll find detailed descriptions of the various research and development projects I've worked on. These posts will cover a range of topics, from fundamental research in computational methods to applied projects in drug discovery and material science.

Each project post will provide an overview of the problem I set out to solve, the methodologies employed, the results obtained, and the implications of these results. My aim is to present this information in a way that's accessible to both specialists in the field and those who might be newer to these topics.

Stay Connected

I plan to regularly update this site with new projects, findings, and insights. If you're interested in staying up-to-date with my work, feel free to check back periodically or connect with me through the contact information provided on this site.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find the content here both informative and inspiring!
